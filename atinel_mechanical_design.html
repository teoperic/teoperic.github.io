Conveyor Line Design

Internship at ATINEL d.o.o. | Varaždin, Croatia

I designed a complete conveyor line from using Autodesk Inventor. This involved creating detailed 3D CAD models of all mechanical components, including frames, rollers, guides, and drive systems. I also produced comprehensive technical drawings and assemblies to support manufacturing and procurement. Moreover, I rendered realistic visualizations of the system. The design was modular and scalable, allowing easy integration into existing production environments. This project showcased my ability to combine mechanical design with practical industrial requirements.
